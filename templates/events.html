Plant Based Ireland Events

For members only!

What are events?

Plant Based Ireland is more than just a website - its a community. Everyone who reads the site, logs in, posts a link or comment, we are all interacting under the umbrella topic of plant based foods in Ireland. We want this community to grow , strengthen and diversify. To this end Plant Based Ireland will be hosting a number of events in the near future. These include online zoom calls with group meetings, competitions, social meetups, trade shows and parties. See below a list of our upcoming events hosted and organised by Plant Based Ireland. If you are interested in contributing to these events, if you want to help in any way, or if you want to host an event of you own, please contact us at plantbased@gmail.com

Online events

  • Weekly hangout - Saturday 16th of September, 12pm Weekly hangouts are chill get togethers just to say hi, hello and of course, how are ya? Feel free to jump on and take part in a casual chat with friends of the Plant Based Ireland community. Tell us a story or get something off your chest. Its right around lunch time so its the perfect time to compare cooking plans for plant based lunches! Meet up via zoom link, link to the zoom meeting will be emailed to registered members the morning of the event

  • Green in the face - Thursday 21st of September, 6.30pm Green in the face is a podcast held by members of the Plant Based Ireland team Jane and Mairead. This week we will be inerviewing Tomás McShallowcast, the newest and hottest vegan chef from Roscommon. There's nothing common about the interesting plant based dishes he has been cooking up though, join us for what should be fascinating discussion on the trials and tribulations of carving out a place for yourself in culinary Ireland. Podcast is not yet available on audio platforms as of yet, a link to the recording will be shared via email to registered members the morning of the event

  • Show us your dish - Sunday 24th of September , 5pm Show us your dish is hosted by Plant Based Ireland team member Frank. Its Frank's time to show off the latest vegan dishes he has been experimenting with in his mad scientist kitchen. Join us on zoom so you can contribute and show off your recent dishes too, with recipes! Meet up via zoom link, link to the zoom meeting will be emailed to registered members the morning of the event
